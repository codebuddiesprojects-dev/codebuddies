01 · About

Turning Ideas Into
A+ Projects

CodeBuddies began as a simple idea during college days — noticing that many students relied on pre-built solutions without truly understanding what they learned. We believed learning should be meaningful, not mechanical.

By 2024, CodeBuddies took shape, and in April 2025 we began operating formally — supporting students and working professionals with project guidance, technical clarity, and structured learning. Today we provide end-to-end support across CSE, ECE, IT, and related fields.
